Volunteers

Anci Mårdh (AFU archivist), born 1949, previously worked with the Norrköping community’s school adminstration. She first worked for AFU as an out-of-work apprentice (“ALU project”) in 1994. In January 2000 she was a pensioner and returned to AFU as a volunteer, the first few years working with our clipping collections. In recent years Anci has been occupied with ordering the Danish report files deposited with AFU by the Scandinavian UFO Information (SUFOI). Anci works three hours each Monday, Tuesday and Wednesday.Anci has a life-long interest in paranormal phenomena and recently moved to a flat conveniently close to AFU headquarters.
Ingrid Collberg  (AFU librarian), born 1941, has worked with the AFU team since September 2008. She is a professional librarian who was chief of cataloguing at the Norrköping community library before going into pension. Ingrid classifies and catalogues new book collections to our two libraries. She has the perfect background for the current work on old antiquarian books we received through the Hilary Evans library. Before retiring she had worked for many years cataloguing on the community’s so called Finspong collection of 35.000 old books from the 17th and 18th centuries. Ingrid works for us on Mondays and Tuesdays.
