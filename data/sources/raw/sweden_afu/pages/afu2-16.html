Donors

 

Throughout the years AFU has received hundreds of collections as donations (and in some cases as deposits or outright acquisitions against money).To make a complete list of all this is nearly impossible. One of the raw measures we can use is the number of new book titles/editions that each donor contributed to our shelves, but in the list below we have also included collections of note not only because they included books. Some special collections are also mentioned in the sub-pages from the Collections main menu. We are now (2021-2022) working on adding & describing all substantial donations received in the last decade!
